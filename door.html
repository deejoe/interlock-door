Go to

http://deejoe.sdf.org/interlock

right click on door.asc and Save as ... or otherwise download the file door.asc and save it to your computer.

Or, open a terminal emulator on a desktop or laptop GNU/Linux system (xterm gnome-terminal rxvt or whatever), or on Mac (Terminal), and issue:

wget http://deejoe.sdf.org/interlock/door.asc

or

curl -O http://deejoe.sdf.org/interlock/door.asc

In all cases, navigate in a terminal emulator to where your copy of door.asc has been saved, and then issue:

gpg -d door.asc

This will prompt you for a password and, once the password has been entered, should display to you the file contents.
